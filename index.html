Most AI Implementations Are Built to Break

Your developer built a system that works today. But AI infrastructure is different from traditional software — models update, APIs change, costs are unpredictable.

What Usually Breaks

Model deprecations. Your system calls GPT-3.5-turbo. OpenAI sunsets it. Your app stops working overnight.
Rate limits. You hit peak traffic. Requests start failing. Customers see error messages.
Cost explosions. Token usage spikes. Your $500/month bill becomes $5,000. No warning, no control.
Prompt brittleness. The model updates. Your carefully tuned prompts now produce garbage.
Vendor lock-in. You want to switch from OpenAI to Anthropic? Complete rewrite required.

These aren't edge cases. They're inevitable outcomes of fragile architecture.
